8 Lost chunks Recovery Set (LRS) of Rules

  • Context of use: The splitter can lost some chunks.
  • Clue: Massively lost chunks (those that have not arrived to none of the monitor peers) are re-sent by the splitter (usually to one or more monitor peers).
